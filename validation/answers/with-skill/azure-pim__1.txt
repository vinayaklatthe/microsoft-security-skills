Set activation controls proportionate to the sensitivity of the role - tighter for Global Admin and other high-impact roles.

- Require approval: a designated approver must grant the elevation before it takes effect for the most privileged roles.
- Require strong auth: require MFA or a specific authentication strength (phishing-resistant for the top roles) at the point of activation.
- Require justification: make the admin enter a business justification.
- Require ticket info: capture a ticket/change number so activations tie back to a work item.
- Set a maximum activation duration: keep the window short (for example a few hours) so elevated access expires automatically.

Combine these with recurring access reviews and PIM alerts so activations are both controlled at the moment of use and reviewed over time.
