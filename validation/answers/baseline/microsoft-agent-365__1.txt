To govern and secure third-party and Copilot Studio agents from one place:

- Keep a central list of all agents regardless of where they were built.
- Apply consistent policies and access controls across them.
- Give each agent appropriate permissions and review access regularly.
- Monitor activity and audit what the agents do.
- Make sure data the agents can access is protected.

Having a single governance approach helps you reduce risk and stay compliant as the number of agents grows.
