To remediate oversharing across Microsoft 365 before a new data program:

- Review who has access to your SharePoint sites, OneDrive, and Teams, and look for sites shared too broadly.
- Tighten permissions on sensitive sites and remove access that isn't needed.
- Apply sensitivity labels to classify and protect important content.
- Clean up broad sharing and reduce the use of "everyone" type permissions.
- Set up default sharing settings that are more restrictive.
- Keep monitoring access over time so things don't drift.

Reducing over-broad access and labelling sensitive content will lower your exposure before you turn on the new program.
