To stop oversharing from creeping back in SharePoint, set up some guardrails:

- Use sensitivity labels and container labels to protect sites and content.
- Configure default sharing settings to be more restrictive for new sites.
- Run periodic access reviews and clean up inactive sites.
- Educate site owners on good sharing practices.
- Monitor sharing activity so you can catch issues early.

Putting defaults and reviews in place helps keep permissions under control as your environment grows.
