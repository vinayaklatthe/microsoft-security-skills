To remove standing Global Administrator access, move privileged access to Microsoft Entra Privileged Identity Management and make elevation temporary, approved, and monitored.

1. Prepare emergency access
- Maintain at least two cloud-only emergency access accounts.
- Protect them with strong credentials and monitor their sign-ins.
- Exclude them only from policies that could lock you out.

2. Inventory privileged assignments
- List all active and eligible assignments for Global Administrator, Privileged Role Administrator, Security Administrator, Application Administrator, Cloud Application Administrator, Exchange Administrator, SharePoint Administrator, Intune Administrator, and Conditional Access Administrator.
- Identify users with permanent active assignments.

3. Convert standing admins to eligible
- In PIM, change permanent active Global Administrator assignments to eligible assignments.
- Use least privilege roles instead of Global Administrator wherever possible.
- Remove accounts that do not need the role.

4. Configure activation requirements
- Require MFA on activation.
- Require justification.
- Require approval for the most sensitive roles.
- Require a ticket number if your process uses one.
- Use short activation durations such as one to four hours.
- Notify security administrators when high-privilege roles are activated.

5. Limit who can manage privilege
- Keep Privileged Role Administrator tightly controlled.
- Use access reviews for eligible assignments.
- Remove stale guests, service accounts, and shared accounts from privileged roles.

6. Monitor and review
- Alert on role assignment changes, PIM activation, permanent assignment creation, and emergency account sign-in.
- Review PIM history and audit logs regularly.
- Run recurring access reviews for privileged roles.

7. Roll out safely
- Pilot with a small admin group.
- Confirm admins can activate and perform their work.
- Then remove remaining standing Global Administrator assignments.

The end state should be no daily-use standing Global Administrators, just-in-time activation through PIM, and emergency access accounts reserved for break-glass only.
